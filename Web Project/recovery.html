Physical Therapy

Physical therapy, often abbreviated as PT, is a healthcare profession and a form of rehabilitation therapy that focuses on helping individuals restore and improve their physical function, mobility, and quality of life. Physical therapists, also known as physiotherapists in some countries.

Massage Therapy

Massage therapy is a hands-on manipulation of the body's soft tissues, primarily the muscles, to promote relaxation, alleviate pain, improve circulation, and enhance overall well-being. It is a holistic approach to healthcare that has been practiced for thousands of years in various cultures around the world.

Nutritional Counseling

Nutritional counseling is a specialized form of healthcare that involves working with a trained nutrition professional, such as a registered dietitian or nutritionist, to assess and improve an individual's dietary habits and overall nutritional well-being. It aims to provide personalized guidance and education to help people make informed and healthier food choices

Yoga

Yoga is a holistic practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, meditation, and ethical principles to promote overall health and well-being. It originated in ancient India and has been practiced for thousands of years. Yoga encompasses various styles and approaches, but its primary aim is to achieve harmony between the mind, body, and spirit.

Chiropractic Care

Chiropractic care is a healthcare profession and alternative medicine approach that focuses on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal disorders, primarily those related to the spine. Chiropractors, who are licensed healthcare professionals, use manual manipulation and other non-invasive techniques to address issues like back pain, neck pain and headaches.
